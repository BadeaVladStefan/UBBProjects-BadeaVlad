About Formula 1

Formula 1 is the highest class of international auto racing for single-seater formula racing cars sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The World Championship has been one of the premier forms of racing around the world since its inaugural season in 1950.

Each season consists of a series of races, known as Grands Prix, which take place worldwide on purpose-built circuits and public roads. The results of each race are combined to determine two annual World Championships: one for drivers and one for constructors.

Max Verstappen

Max Emilian Verstappen is a Belgian-Dutch racing driver who competes in Formula 1 with Red Bull Racing. He was born on September 30, 1997, in Hasselt, Belgium. Verstappen made his debut in Formula 1 at the 2015 Australian Grand Prix with Scuderia Toro Rosso, becoming the youngest driver to compete in Formula 1 at the age of 17.

Known for his aggressive driving style and exceptional talent, Verstappen quickly rose to prominence in Formula 1. He joined Red Bull Racing in 2016 and has since become one of the most successful drivers on the grid.

Verstappen has won multiple races and championships, solidifying his place as one of the top drivers in the sport.
